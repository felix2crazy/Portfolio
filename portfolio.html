The Programmer Journey

Back in the room after my work was done on a boring morning I took out my brother's hp Laptop, and went through his folders and stumbled on a particular one named "PROGRAMMING". I felt pulled inward, toward something that would define me today. I went through the folder, watched the videos and read the things written about coding in general. I knew what coding was. I needed to know more, a needle for a hungry vein. There's a palpable simmering whenever I am around code nowadays, as if limitless potential is in there, churning, trying to escape (and it probabaly is).
